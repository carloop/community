@reporter500,

Ah, now I understand your question from the other thread …

The RedbearDuo works just fine in the Particle environment. I have one myself and it works fine.
The one thing you cannot do in the Particle environment is to update the system firmware on the Duo; you have to do that through the files in the Duo github repository.

The part you are probably missing are the instructions to setup and claim the RedbearDuo to your Particle account. I found this step was tricky and the Redbear app does not match its documentation. I had to try it a few times before it worked.

The instructions are here:

github.com
