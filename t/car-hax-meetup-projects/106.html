Our next Car Hax Meetup is on September, 14 at Root.vc in SF.
@sahilpotnis and @parin.dedhia you mentioned you might be able to bring a Honda ECU and an instrument cluster from a junkyard? If you do, please ask the junkyard you are buying it from to leave a pigtail connector (see next paragraph). You can go to car-part.com and search for the ECU and Instrument Cluster (dashboard) of your exact Honda and they’ll pull the part and ship it to you. It would be very good to test this and we can help you build your test stand.

Make sure you specifically request that they leave 6 to 8 inches of “pigtail wiring harness” on both the ECU and instrument cluster so it comes with the connectors and wires. Thanks guys!!

Also, make sure you order a 12V power supply. I highly recommend this one but any 12V power supply will work.
