alanm September 10, 2019, 1:21pm 2

The only boards currently supported are the Electron, Photon and Redbear Duo. You can buy a regular Carloop (Carloop Particle Adapter for OBD II), and separately purchase an Electron from Particle. That is equivalent to Carloop 3G (which is currently out of stock as a kit).

If you’d like GPS capability, you can purchase Carloop Pro which comes with the Electron + GPS and SD card reader.
